Northwoods Inn Purple Cabbage Salad

Description

The restaurant Northwoods Inn or Clearmans serves this slaw. This salad is delish!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head red cabbage, cored
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Steps

  1. Shred the cabbage so it is irregular, with some fine and some coarse shreds, and place in a large nonaluminum bowl.
  2. Combine the oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, seasoned salt, pepper and onion powder. Add to the cabbage and mix well.
  3. Cover the salad and let stand several hours to allow the flavors to mellow and the cabbage to achieve a deep red color.
